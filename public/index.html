Reaction Networks
Automation in one occupation creates cascades — it reduces demand for upstream roles, accelerates displacement in structurally similar occupations, and sometimes increases demand for complementary roles. These are the reaction pathways of AI political economy.
Predictions & Validation
VALENCE is a scientific classification system, not a visualization. It stores falsifiable predictions against future data releases. A framework that quietly rewrites history is not a forecasting system — this page is the accountability mechanism.
